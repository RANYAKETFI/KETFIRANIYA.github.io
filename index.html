AI Integration Framework for Wearable Thermal Sensors

Role: PhD Researcher ‚Äî lead author, state-of-the-art analysis, framework design.

Problem: Wearable thermography devices show promise for breast cancer detection, but lack standardized AI pipelines, robust data preparation strategies, and clear paths toward clinical translation.

Method: Conducted a structured narrative review of wearable devices with thermal sensors and proposed a three-phase AI integration framework: (1) data preparation, (2) model development, (3) model evaluation.
